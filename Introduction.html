Introduction 

I think teaching is an art and there are born teachers. But there are majority of teachers, who can improve upon by experience of practice and utilization of various methods of teaching. The basic aim of teaching any subject is to bring about desired change in behaviour. The change in behaviour of child will be indicated through children’s capacity to learn effectively. This is only possible by adopting various methods of teaching. 

The teacher cannot utilize any method to any type of students in any type of environment. He/She has to choose and adopt the right method of teaching keeping in mind the capability of the learners and the curriculum. Thus, method is a way of presentation of the content in the classroom. But it is however very important to keep in mind that a method is not an end itself but is used to achieve the set aims of teaching.

You should also keep in mind that, same method should not be used at all times but there should be flexibility in using it base on situations, circumstances, and condition in a particular case. You should use various methods depending upon demand of the situation. The method use effectively by you in a particular class under a particular circumstance, may be a total failure for other teachers. However, some set criteria for selection of a method of teaching can be considered when selecting as illustrated below.

Principles for selecting methods

There are some guiding principles for determining teaching methods. They are as follows: -


1. Principle of sense of achievement through interest and purpose.

2. Principle of active cooperation.

3. Principle of capability of students of particular class.

4. Principle of realization of meaning of education i.e., “I bring up”, “I nourish”, “Drawing art”.

5. Psychological principle i.e., need, interest of students, etc.

6. Principle of individual difference i.e., different potentialities of students.
